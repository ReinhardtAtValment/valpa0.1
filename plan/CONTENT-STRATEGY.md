### Service 1: Process Analytics & Continuous Improvement

**Purpose:**
Establish a repeatable, data-driven improvement loop for business-critical processes.

**Typical Context:**
- SAP-driven core processes (O2C, P2P, service, logistics, manufacturing)
- Existing or planned use of process mining, Signavio, BI, or data platforms
- Improvement initiatives driven by intuition rather than evidence

**What This Delivers:**
- Clear visibility into actual process execution
- Identified bottlenecks, rework, and structural inefficiencies
- A prioritized improvement backlog (including automation and AI candidates)
- Simple KPIs and monitoring for ongoing control

**How It Works (High Level):**
- Extract and structure event data from SAP and surrounding systems
- Analyze flows, variants, delays, and exceptions
- Translate findings into concrete improvement initiatives
- Set up a lightweight review cadence to track outcomes

**Value:**
- Moves discussions from opinion to fact
- Creates transparency for management and process owners
- Enables continuous improvement beyond one-off analysis

**Tooling:**
SAP Signavio, SAP Business Data Cloud, Databricks, BI tools, custom event analytics

**What This Delivers:**

**Value:**
- Lower long-term maintenance effort

### Headline: Our Methodology

**Subheadline:**
Whether working on process optimization, SAP modernization, or AI governance, we follow a consistent approach that prioritizes business value and ensures execution.

---

### Discovery Sprints (4-6 Weeks)

**Purpose:**
Identify and prioritize improvement opportunities through data-driven analysis.

**What Happens:**
- Extract and analyze operational data (process logs, system events, transactions)
- Identify bottlenecks, waste, rework, and automation candidates
- Build a prioritized portfolio of improvements with estimated business value
- Define ownership, KPIs, and next steps

**Deliverables:**
- Process maps and analysis
- Improvement opportunity list (ranked by value vs. effort)
- Initial roadmap for next 3-6 months
- Lightweight governance framework

**Typical Engagement:**
4-6 weeks, fixed scope, focused on one business area or process

**Best For:**
- Organizations new to process analytics
- Testing approach before larger commitment
- Specific pain points that need immediate attention

---

### Fractional Leadership (Ongoing)

**Purpose:**
Maintain and govern improvement portfolios to ensure continuous value delivery.

**What Happens:**
- Regular portfolio reviews (bi-weekly or monthly)
- Track KPIs and initiative progress
- Resolve blockers and manage risks
- Maintain architecture and governance alignment
- Guide teams on prioritization and execution

**Typical Commitment:**
0.5-2 days per week, 3-12 month engagements

**Best For:**
- Organizations with multiple improvement initiatives
- Need for ongoing architecture or governance oversight
- Building internal capability over time

---

### Business Value Transparency

**Our Differentiator:**
We make business value visible and trackable.

**How:**
- Every initiative has clear value metrics (time saved, cost reduced, errors prevented)
- Regular tracking of before/after results
- Portfolio dashboards showing delivered value vs. pipeline
- Honest assessment of what's working and what's not

**Why It Matters:**
Many organizations have lots of analysis and plans, but struggle to connect them to measurable outcomes. We keep value front and center throughout every engagement.
