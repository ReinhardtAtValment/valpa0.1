## Brand Name vs Legal Entity

### Brand Name: **Valment**
This is the public-facing brand name used across all marketing, website, and customer communications.

**Usage:**
- ✅ Website header/logo
- ✅ Marketing materials
- ✅ Social media
- ✅ Customer communications
- ✅ Product/service descriptions
- ✅ Meta titles and descriptions
- ✅ Casual references

**Examples:**
- "Welcome to Valment"
- "Valment helps organizations..."
- "Contact Valment for..."
- Website title: "Valment | Process Optimization & AI Governance"

---

### Legal Entity: **Valment Partners Inc.**
This is the registered legal business name used for official documents, contracts, and legal requirements.

**Location:** Oakville, Ontario, Canada

**Usage:**
- ✅ Copyright notices
- ✅ Legal documents
- ✅ Contracts
- ✅ Privacy policies
- ✅ Terms of service
- ✅ Official business correspondence
- ✅ Invoices and financial documents

**Examples:**
- "© 2025 Valment Partners Inc. All rights reserved."
- "Valment Partners Inc., a company incorporated in Ontario..."
- Contract signatures: "Valment Partners Inc."

---

### Future Entity: **Valment Solutions Inc.**
Planned US entity - will follow same branding pattern

**Location:** United States (TBD)

**Brand Name:** Still "Valment" (consistent across entities)  
**Legal Name:** "Valment Solutions Inc."

**Rationale:** Using one brand name ("Valment") across multiple legal entities simplifies brand recognition and customer experience.

## Messaging Guidelines

### First Reference in Documents
When introducing the brand in official documents:

**Option 1 (Casual):**
"Valment is a process optimization and AI governance consulting firm..."

**Option 2 (Formal):**
"Valment Partners Inc. (doing business as Valment) is a process optimization..."

### Subsequent References
Always use "Valment" after first formal reference

### Client-Facing
Always use "Valment" - simpler, more memorable, easier to say

## Multi-Entity Strategy

### Rationale for Single Brand Name

**Benefits:**
1. **Brand Consistency:** One brand across all markets
2. **Customer Clarity:** No confusion about "Partners" vs "Solutions"
3. **Scalability:** Easy to add more entities (countries) under one brand
4. **Marketing Efficiency:** Single brand to build and promote
5. **Recognition:** Memorable 3-syllable name

**Structure:**
```
VALMENT (Brand)
├── Valment Partners Inc. (Canada)
└── Valment Solutions Inc. (US - Future)
```

### Regional Variations
If needed for specific markets, can use:
- "Valment Canada"
- "Valment US"

But default is simply: **Valment**

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Use "Valment Partners" in marketing or headers
- Mix brand name and legal name inconsistently
- Forget "Inc." in legal documents
- Use different brand names per entity

✅ **Do:**
- Use "Valment" for all customer-facing content
- Use "Valment Partners Inc." in legal/official contexts
- Be consistent across all materials
- Update future entities to follow same pattern
