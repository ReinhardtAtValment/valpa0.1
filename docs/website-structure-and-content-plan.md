**Purpose:** Create immediate clarity about value proposition and build credibility

**Hero Visual Suggestions:**

#### Value Proposition Section
**Headline:** "From Process Discovery to Portfolio Governance"

**Three Core Pillars:**

1. **Discover**
   - Use process discovery to see how work really flows
   - Identify waste, bottlenecks, and optimization opportunities
   - Uncover AI agent use cases within your processes
   - Build evidence-based improvement portfolios

2. **Prioritize**
   - Transparent business value modeling for each opportunity
   - Portfolio management approach across improvements and AI agents
   - Balance quick wins with strategic AI initiatives
   - Clear governance framework for AI agent deployment

3. **Govern & Control**
   - Ongoing portfolio management and control
   - AI agent governance and risk management
   - Continuous measurement and optimization
   - Transparent ROI tracking across all initiatives

#### Problem/Solution Section
**Headline:** "The Challenge Most Organizations Face"

**Problems (with brief descriptions):**
- Lots of process improvement ideas, no systematic way to prioritize
- AI and automation opportunities identified but unclear how to govern them safely
- Process optimization tools underutilized
- No portfolio view across improvements, corrections, and AI agents
- Lack of governance framework for managing AI agents in business processes

**The Valment Difference:**
We combine process discovery expertise with AI agent governance to create a managed portfolio approach. Every improvement, correction, and AI agent opportunity is evaluated, prioritized, and governed—ensuring you optimize processes while safely deploying AI.
